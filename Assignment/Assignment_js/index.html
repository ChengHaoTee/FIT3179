Where is more happy to live?

The worldwide average happiness score in 2020 is 5.47, we can see that it is not bad to live in North America and ANZ, Asutralia and New Zealand and the regions that have above-average score.

Happiness Score v.s. CPI

Is there relationship between Happiness Score and Preception of Corruption? The people happier region like Western Europe has lower CPI whereas the people in Sub-Saharan Africa are more aware of the corruption of their public sectors.

The Happier The Better?

There is a observable trend that higher happiness score would increase the healthy life expectancy . However, it does not means that the people living in happier area would have more freedom to make life choice!
