World Happiness Report

Happy People! Where to live?

Which Part Of The World Is The Happiest?

Among the top 25 happiest countries in the world (based on 2020 data), 15 of them are located at Western Europe. From year 2016 to 2020, majority of the top 10 happiest countries are located in Western Europe.

Happiness Score Among The Regions

The worldwide average happiness score in 2020 is 5.47. Australia and New Zealand has the highest average happiness score, however the country with maximum score is located in Western Europe.

Happiness Score v.s. Corruption Preceptions Index

There relationship between Happiness Score and Preception of Corruption! The people in happier region like Western Europe has lower CPI whereas the people in Sub-Saharan Africa are more aware of the corruption of their public sectors.

The Happier The Better?

There is observable trend that higher happiness score would have better healthy life expectancy. However, it does not means that the people living in happier area would have more freedom to make life choice!
